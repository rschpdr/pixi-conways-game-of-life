Help
  • The simplest way to start a simulation is selecting the "Random" button above and then "Start"
  • The cells are interactable, try drawing something in them and clicking "Start". Or maybe check existing patterns
  • "Stop" will pause the simulation without clearing the world
  • "Reset" will pause the simulation AND clear the world (reset all cells to "dead" state)
  • Below the grid there are some controls to customize the look of the world. Be sure to click "Apply" to see the changes. WARNING: "Apply" WILL reset the world (killing all live cells).
  • Depending on how many rows and columns you choose, you may face layout issues (horizontal scrollbars) or performance issues ("jagged" animations or frameskips)
